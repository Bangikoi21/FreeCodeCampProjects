Michelle Trachtenburg

A tribute to an actress gone too soon

Michelle Trachtenburg, the young actress and spokesperson for RADD

All Things Michelle

Michelle Christine Trachtenberg was born on October 11, 1985

She played in Buffy the vampire slayer from 2000 to 2003 that won her the Young Actress Award and earned her three Saturn Award nominations.

She played in more movies such as Euro Trip, Gossip Girl, Ice Princess, and more.

She was raised in Sheepshead Bay Brooklyn, where she attended The Bay Academy for the Arts and Sciences

She was a youth representative for the launch of an anti-drug campaign with President Bill Clinton

"Michelle was one of a kind. I remember her coming on set for the first time and just absolutely owning it. She was a force of nature and just so so unapologetically funny and magnetic" said Chace Crawford, a former co-star.
