Operation

Before the initial use, please ensure thorough washing of all parts that came into contact with food

Please use the following combination:

Minced Meat (this function can be used for mincing meat and vegetables)

  1. Align bosses on both sides of the upper blade with the sliding grooves
  2. Place minced meat blade holder in the minced meat cup, hten add the ingredients to the cup
  3. Align the handle with that of the glass bowl, then securely close the lid

Stirring is intended for making juice, jam, and milkshakes

  1. Cut the ingredients into 1.5mm pieces, placing them in the mixing cup, add a suitable amount of water and milk, then tighten the mixing blade
  2. Press button on the side of the whole machine, open the upper cover, and then rotate the conversion board clockwise into place
  3. Tighten the mixing cup into the unit
